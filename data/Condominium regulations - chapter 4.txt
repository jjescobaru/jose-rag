### **INTERNAL REGULATIONS FOR COEXISTENCE AND ADMINISTRATION**
**FOR THE VERTICAL CONDOMINIUM "TRIVENTO III"**
**Last Updated:** December 2025
**Effective Date:** From January 1, 2026

---

#### **CHAPTER IV: TECHNOLOGY, DIGITAL SECURITY AND PRIVACY**
**ARTICLE 14°.- VIDEO SURVEILLANCE AND DIGITAL ACCESS SYSTEM.**
* 14.1. Video surveillance images are for the exclusive security of the condominium. Access is restricted to the Administrator and the Chairman of the Board.
* 14.2. **Request for Recordings:** A co-owner may request a review of recordings in writing, specifying the date, approximate time, and justified reason (theft, damage). The administration may deny vague requests or those that invade the privacy of third parties.
* 14.3. **Personal Data:** The condominium will be governed by the Personal Data Protection Act. The information of owners and tenants (telephone, email, license plate) will be treated confidentially and used only for administrative purposes.

**ARTICLE 15°.- OFFICIAL APPLICATION AND DIGITAL COMMUNICATIONS.**
* 15.1. The condominium will implement an official application for: payment of expenses, reservations, reporting of news, circulation of minutes and communications.
* 15.2. It is the co-owner's responsibility to keep their contact details up to date in the application/inbox.

**ARTICLE 16°.- ELECTRICAL AND FIBER OPTIC INSTALLATIONS.**
* 16.1. Any private installation of cables, antennas or routers that affect the facade or common areas must be pre-approved by the administration.
* 16.2. Tampering with common electrical panels or internet/fiber optic distribution points in the building is prohibited.
