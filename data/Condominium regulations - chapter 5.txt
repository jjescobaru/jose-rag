### **INTERNAL REGULATIONS FOR COEXISTENCE AND ADMINISTRATION**
**FOR THE VERTICAL CONDOMINIUM "TRIVENTO III"**
**Last Updated:** December 2025
**Effective Date:** From January 1, 2026

---

#### **CHAPTER V: SPECIAL REGIME FOR SHORT-TERM RENTALS (AIRBNB AND SIMILAR)**
**ARTICLE 17째.- DEFINITION AND PRIOR AUTHORIZATION.**
* 17.1. Short Term Rental (STR) is considered to be the transfer of use of a private unit for periods of less than 30 days, through digital platforms or direct contracts.
* 17.2. **Mandatory Authorization:** Any owner wishing to carry out ACD must notify the Administration and the Board of Directors in writing, providing the contact details of the local person in charge (who must reside in the city).

**ARTICLE 18째.- OBLIGATIONS OF THE OWNER AND THE GUEST.**
* 18.1. **Absolute Responsibility:** The owner is responsible for all actions, debts and damages caused by his guests, as well as the payment of any fines they incur.
* 18.2. **Guest Registration:** The owner or their local representative must register the details (name, identity document, check-in and check-out date) of ALL guests at the reception desk or in the application before their arrival.
*   18.3. **Delivery of Regulations:** A digital or physical copy of these regulations (relevant chapters) must be delivered to short-term rental guests (AirBnB) at check-in.
* 18.4. **Occupant Limit:** The unit may not accommodate more people than its square footage allows (maximum 2 people per bedroom + 2 additional people).

**ARTICLE 19째.- SPECIFIC RULES FOR ACD GUESTS.**
* 19.1. **Prohibition of Parties and Events:** It is strictly forbidden for ACD guests to organize parties, gatherings with more than double the capacity of the unit, or events in the unit that cause disturbances.
* 19.2. **Use of Common Areas:** ACD guests have the right to use the common areas (pool,(barbecue grill)on the same terms as residents, but without priority. Their access may be restricted during peak hours.

**ARTICLE 20째.- PENALTIES FOR NON-COMPLIANCE WITH THE ACD REGIME.**
* 20.1. **First Serious Offense** (party, excessive noise, excess occupants): Fine for the owner of US$ 300.
* 20.2. **Recidivism (2nd Serious Offense):** Fine of US$ 500 and suspension of the right to perform ACD for a period of 3 months.
* 20.3. **Third Serious Offense:** Fine of US$ 1,000 and suspension of the right to conduct ACD for a period of 6 months. The Board may initiate legal action for damages to coexistence.
