### **INTERNAL REGULATIONS FOR COEXISTENCE AND ADMINISTRATION**
**FOR THE VERTICAL CONDOMINIUM "TRIVENTO III"**
**Last Updated:** December 2025
**Effective Date:** From January 1, 2026

---

#### **CHAPTER VII: MOVING, MAJOR WORKS AND REPAIRS**
*(The process is detailed)*
**ARTICLE 23°.- MOVING PROTOCOL.**
* 23.1. Notification 5 days in advance. Signing of liability document for damages.
* 23.2. **Strict Hours:** Monday to Friday 9:00-17:00. **No service on Saturday afternoons, Sundays, and holidays.**
*   23.3. **Mandatory Protection:** The movers must place protective covers on floors, walls, corners, and elevators. Penalty for damage: repair cost + US$ 500.

**ARTICLE 24°.- INTERIOR WORKS AND RENOVATIONS.**
* 24.1. **Project Approved:** Submission of plans and schedule to the administration. Security deposit for damages to common areas (minimum US$ 2,000).
* 24.2. **Debris Management:** The contractor must remove debris daily in designated containers. Use of regular garbage bins is prohibited.
* 24.3. **Civil Liability:** The owner must submit a copy of the contractor's civil liability insurance.
