**Last Updated:** December 2025
**Effective Date:** From January 1, 2026

---

#### **CHAPTER IX: DISCIPLINARY REGIME AND MEDIATION**
**ARTICLE 28°.- GRADUATION OF OFFENSES AND SANCTIONS.**
* **Minor Offense** (e.g., improper disposal of trash, first time): Written warning.
* **Serious Offense** (e.g., noise during quiet hours, damage to common property, non-compliance with ACD): Economic fine (US$ 200 - US$ 1,000) according to the attached table.
* **Very Serious Offense** (e.g., physical assault, vandalism, illegal activities): Fine up to US$ 5,000, suspension of rights to common areas, and referral to competent authorities.

**ARTICLE 29°.- SANCTIONING PROCEDURE.**
1. Complaint or finding.
2. Notification to the alleged offender, who has 3 days to submit written defenses.
3. Analysis by the Coexistence Committee (3 members of the board) or the full Board.
4. Reasoned decision and notification.
5. Appeal for reconsideration before the Owners' Assembly.

**ARTICLE 30°.- COMMUNITY RELATIONS COMMITTEE.**
A committee of 3 owners elected annually is created, which will act as the first instance for mediation of neighborhood conflicts before escalating to a sanction.
