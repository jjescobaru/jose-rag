### **INTERNAL REGULATIONS FOR COEXISTENCE AND ADMINISTRATION**
**FOR THE VERTICAL CONDOMINIUM "TRIVENTO III"**
**Last Updated:** December 2025
**Effective Date:** From January 1, 2026

---


#### **CHAPTER VIII: PERSONNEL, SERVICES AND EMERGENCY PROTOCOLS**
**ARTICLE 25°.- FUNCTIONS OF THE ADMINISTRATOR (Expanded).**
It includes financial management, execution of assembly agreements, supervision of contractors, conflict mediation, and presentation of detailed quarterly reports.

**ARTICLE 26°.- EMERGENCY PROTOCOL.**
* 26.1. **Evacuation Route and Meeting Point:** Plans located on each floor. Meeting point: [SPECIFY, e.g., South corner].
* 26.2. **First Aid Kit and Fire Extinguishers:** It is prohibited to use fire extinguishers in common areas for non-emergency purposes. Fine: US$ 500 + recharge cost.

**ARTICLE 27°.- RELATIONSHIP WITH PERSONNEL (Guards, Cleaning).**
* 27.1. **Mandatory Respect:** Dignified and respectful treatment is required. Complaints about staff must be submitted in writing to management, not handled directly.
* 27.2. **Prohibition of Constant Tips/Gifts:** To avoid favoritism and labor conflicts, it is requested that the habit of periodic tips or gifts to plant personnel not be established.
