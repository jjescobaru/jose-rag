### **INTERNAL REGULATIONS FOR COEXISTENCE AND ADMINISTRATION**
**FOR THE VERTICAL CONDOMINIUM "TRIVENTO III"**
**Last Updated:** December 2025
**Effective Date:** From January 1, 2026

---

#### **CHAPTER II: RULES OF COEXISTENCE, PUBLIC HEALTH AND SUSTAINABILITY**
**ARTICLE 8°.- NOISE AND SOUND DISTURBANCES.**
* 8.1. **Quiet Hours:** These are established from Monday to Friday from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM, and on Saturdays, Sundays, and holidays from 12:00 AM to 9:00 AM. During these periods, the noise level inside the units must not be audible from the hallway or neighboring units.
* 8.2. The use of drills, hammers and noisy machinery is prohibited outside the hours of 09:00 to 12:30 and 15:00 to 18:00 hrs, Monday to Friday. Prohibited on weekends and holidays.
* 8.3. The use of headphones and acoustic attenuation in home theaters is encouraged. Fine: US$ 300 - US$ 500 for non-compliance.

**ARTICLE 10°.- ENERGY AND WATER EFFICIENCY.**
* 10.1. It is everyone's responsibility to optimize consumption. The use of LED technology and A+ energy-efficient appliances is recommended.
* 10.2. Dripping or obvious waste of water in common areas is prohibited. Report leaks immediately to management.
* 10.3. Common areas will have lighting with motion sensors and timers.


**ARTICLE 11°.- HEALTH AND HYGIENE PROTOCOLS.**
* 11.1. In the event of contagious diseases declared by health authorities, the affected co-owner must inform the administration (confidentially) to activate enhanced cleaning protocols in circulation areas.
* 11.2. Spitting in gardens, hallways or elevators is prohibited. Fine: US$ 200.
