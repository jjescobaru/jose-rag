### **INTERNAL REGULATIONS FOR COEXISTENCE AND ADMINISTRATION**
**FOR THE VERTICAL CONDOMINIUM "TRIVENTO III"**
**Last Updated:** December 2025
**Effective Date:** From January 1, 2026

---

#### **FINAL PROVISIONS AND ANNEXES**
**ARTICLE 31°.- MODIFICATION OF THE REGULATIONS.**
Any modification to these regulations will require the approval of a qualified majority (2/3) of the owners present at an extraordinary General Assembly convened for that purpose.

**ARTICLE 32°.- EFFECTIVENESS AND REPEAL.**
This regulation comes into effect on January 1, 2026.
