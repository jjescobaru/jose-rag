#### **CHAPTER III: USE OF COMMON AREAS, POOL AND SOCIAL AREAS**
*(The original articles are expanded with more detail)*
**ARTICLE 12°.- RESERVATIONS AND USE OF SOCIAL AREAS (POOL, BARBECUE AREA).**
*   12.1. If owners or tenants require the use of the barbecue grill or wish to hold an event, they may use this service by informing the Administration beforehand and paying a fee of US$ 100.00, which will be used for cleaning and maintenance of the space. In case of any damage, the responsible party will be responsible for the repair or replacement of any affected items.
*   12.2. **Strict Hours:** Sundays to Thursdays until 11:00pm. Fridays and Saturdays until 2:00am.

**ARTICLE 13°.- SPECIFIC STANDARDS FOR SWIMMING POOLS.**
* 13.1. **Shower Rule:** It is mandatory to shower before entering the pool.
* 13.2. **Glass Prohibition:** Glass containers are strictly prohibited in the pool area. Only plastics or unbreakable materials are permitted.
* 13.3. **Children:** Children under 12 years of age may only enter accompanied and under the permanent supervision of a responsible adult.
* 13.4. **Cameras:** The pool area is under video surveillance for security. It is not an area of ​​absolute privacy.
