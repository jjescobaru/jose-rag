### **INTERNAL REGULATIONS FOR COEXISTENCE AND ADMINISTRATION**
**FOR THE VERTICAL CONDOMINIUM "TRIVENTO III"**
**Last Updated:** December 2025
**Effective Date:** From January 1, 2026

---

#### **CHAPTER I: ADMINISTRATION AND FINANCE**
**ARTICLE 6°.- COMMON EXPENSES AND SPECIAL FEES.**
* 6.1. Payment of the ordinary monthly expense, corresponding to drinking water, and any special fee approved by the Assembly, is mandatory and is due on the 5th of each month.
* 6.2. **Late Payments:** A 5% surcharge will be applied starting on the 6th. From the second month of debt, a fixed penalty of US$ 30 per month overdue will be applied, in addition to the cumulative surcharge.
* 6.3. **Service Interruption:** After the third month of non-payment, the potable water supply will be cut off and, in extreme cases, access to common areas and electronic systems (cards, applications) will be restricted. Reconnection will require full payment of the outstanding balance, plus a disconnection/reconnection fee of US$ 150.
* 6.4. **Reserve Fund:** 15% of the ordinary monthly expenses will be allocated to a Reserve Fund for major repairs and unforeseen events. Its use will require authorization from the Board of Directors.

**ARTICLE 7°.- NOTIFICATION PROCESS.**
All notifications (warnings, fines, summonses) will be made in a verifiable manner: a) by written communication deposited in the unit's mailbox; b) registered email; c) message through the official condominium application (if one exists). They will be considered received 48 hours after being sent or deposited.
